Detailed Description

True of Date (TOD) frame provider.

Transformation from the Mean of Date (MOD) frame to the True of Date (TOD) frame. This algorithm uses the IAU-76/FK5 theory. Notice that one can provide corrections for the nutation in obliquity and in longitude that are usually obtained from IERS EOP Data.
