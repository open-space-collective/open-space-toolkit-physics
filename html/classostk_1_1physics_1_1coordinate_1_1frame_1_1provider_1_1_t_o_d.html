Detailed Description

True of Date (TOD) frame provider. 

                        Form the matrix of precession-nutation for a given date (including frame bias), IAU 2006
                        precession and IAU 2000A nutation models.

https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Earth-centered_inertial https://www2.mpia-hd.mpg.de/~mathar/progs/sofa_api/group__SF.html
